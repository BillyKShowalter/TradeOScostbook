02 — Color system

03 — Typography
Type system
TradeOS uses two typefaces: a system sans-serif for all display and body text, and Courier New (monospace) for all badge, label, code, and system UI text. The mix of humanist sans and hard-edged monospace is the personality of the brand — professional meets technical.

05 — Usage rules
Do's and don'ts
Do — logo placement
Always use the full lockup (badge + wordmark) when space allows. Keep clearspace equal to the cap-height of the wordmark on all four sides. Use bone white (#F7F2EC) or forge black (#0D0A07) as background only.
Don't — logo placement
Don't place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds. Don't use the wordmark without the badge except in nav bars at small sizes. Don't place the logo on any color other than white, black, or copper.
Do — color usage
Copper (#B87333) is the sole accent color. Use it for the OS tspan, the badge text, CTA buttons, and active states only. Always pair with forge black or bone white as the background surface.
Don't — color usage
Don't recolor the OS tspan to any color other than #B87333. Don't use gradients on the logo or badge. Don't introduce secondary accent colors — the copper is the only color that matters.
Do — typography
Use system sans (400 and 500 weights only) for all headings and body. Use Courier New for all badges, labels, and system UI text. Letter-space all monospace labels at minimum 1.5px.
Don't — typography
Don't use 600 or 700 weight — too heavy against the brand aesthetic. Don't use decorative typefaces. Don't mix monospace and sans within the same text element (the wordmark exception is intentional and specific to the logo mark only).

06 — Brand voice
How TradeOS sounds
The TradeOS voice is direct, honest, and trade-aware. We speak to plumbers and electricians like a knowledgeable peer — not a salesperson, not a tech bro, not a corporate agency. Every word we write respects the fact that our clients are skilled professionals who have no time for fluff.
We are
+Direct — we say what we mean, no padding
+Honest — we don't overpromise or hide the fine print
+Trade-literate — we understand plumbing, HVAC, electrical
+Confident — we know this space and don't hedge
+Midwest-grounded — approachable, not coastal
We are not
—Salesy — no exclamation marks, no hype
—Technical for the sake of it — jargon-free
—Casual to the point of unprofessional
—Corporate — no "synergy", no "solutions"
—Generic — every word should feel trade-specific
Tone examples
AVOID "Unlock the power of digital transformation for your trade business ecosystem!"
PREFERRED "A website that gets you found on Google when a homeowner needs a plumber in Terre Haute."
AVOID "We leverage cutting-edge SEO strategies to maximize your online visibility!"
PREFERRED "We track your rankings monthly and tell you exactly what moved and why."
