# TradeOS Costbook Trade Taxonomy

This document catalogs the standard trade categories supported by the TradeOS Construction Knowledge Engine.

## Core Trade Categories
The taxonomy currently registers the following 24 specialized trade categories:

1. **Cabinetry**: Kitchen, bathroom, and custom built-in cabinets and hardware.
2. **Concrete**: Foundations, footings, structural slabs, and rebar.
3. **Countertops**: Granite, quartz, laminate, and custom solid surface slabs.
4. **Deck**: Wood, composite, and metal decking, joists, and structural posts.
5. **Doors**: Interior, exterior, garage doors, pre-hung frames, and hardware.
6. **Drywall**: Hanging, taping, level 1-5 finishing, and ceiling texturing.
7. **Electrical**: Service panels, rough-in wiring, conduit, fixtures, and devices.
8. **Excavation**: Site clearing, trenching, grading, and backfilling.
9. **Fence**: Wood, vinyl, chain-link, metal fencing, and gates.
10. **Flatwork**: Driveways, patios, walkways, aprons, and stamped concrete.
11. **Flooring**: Carpet, tile, hardwood, engineered wood, laminate, and vinyl.
12. **Framing**: Wood studs, steel studs, headers, joists, trusses, and sheathing.
13. **HVAC**: Furnaces, heat pumps, AC units, ductwork, ventilation, and controls.
14. **Insulation**: Fiberglass batts, blown-in cellulose, spray foam, and vapor barriers.
15. **Painting**: Interior wall, ceiling, exterior siding, trim, and priming.
16. **Plumbing**: Rough-in supply, waste lines, water heaters, and fixtures.
17. **Roofing**: Asphalt shingles, metal panels, underlayment, and flashing.
18. **Siding**: Vinyl, fiber-cement, wood siding, housewrap, and trim.
19. **Trim**: Crown molding, baseboard, casing, and window sills.
20. **Windows**: Vinyl, wood, aluminum windows, screens, and flashing.
21. **General Conditions**: Supervision, cleanup, dumpsters, permits, and mobilization.
22. **Hardware**: Fasteners, brackets, mounting hardware, and locks.
23. **Hardscaping**: Retaining walls, pavers, stone features, and fire pits.
24. **Landscaping**: Sod, soil, mulch, plantings, and tree/brush removal services.

*Note: Newly generated items and assemblies must align with this taxonomy to ensure correct relational mapping during database synchronizations.*
