# TradeOS Construction Knowledge Engine - Assembly Quality Standard

This document establishes the guidelines, quality-control checks, and scoring rules for TradeOS estimating assemblies.

---

## 1. Quality-Control Criteria
Every assembly generated by worker agents must pass through a strict evaluation checking:
1. **Schema Compliance**: Conforms perfectly to `schemas/assembly.schema.json`.
2. **Distinct Scope**: No overlapping names, slugs, or typical use-cases with existing assemblies.
3. **Estimation Value**: Saves time for human estimators by packing logical items (labor + materials + equipment) together.
4. **Input Transparency**: Defines clear parameters (e.g. acces width, pitch, material selection) to scale quantities.
5. **Wording Standard**: High-quality proposal scope of work, warranty language, assumptions, and exclusions.

---

## 2. Assembly Quality Score ($QS$) Formula
The score is calculated out of $1.00$ based on the following weights:

$$QS = w_{schema} \cdot S_{schema} + w_{length} \cdot S_{length} + w_{clauses} \cdot S_{clauses} + w_{inputs} \cdot S_{inputs} + w_{items} \cdot S_{items}$$

Where:
* **Schema Match ($S_{schema}$)**: $1.0$ if all schema fields validate, $0.0$ if errors are present (Weight: $0.30$).
* **Text Length ($S_{length}$)**: Max score if description $> 100$ chars and contractor notes $> 50$ chars (Weight: $0.15$).
* **Legal Clauses ($S_{clauses}$)**: Presence of non-empty `proposalScopeOfWork`, `proposalAssumptions`, `proposalExclusions`, and `warrantyLanguage` (Weight: $0.20$).
* **Required Inputs ($S_{inputs}$)**: Presence of at least two `requiredInputs` parameters (Weight: $0.15$).
* **Cost Book Linkage ($S_{items}$)**: Presence of at least two `lineItems` pointing to valid cost items (Weight: $0.20$).

---

## 3. Thresholds & Action Gates
* **Score $\ge 0.90$**: **Auto-Approve Candidate**. The runner will approve the batch and merge it into the database.
* **Score $0.80$ to $0.89$**: **Human Review Required**. The runner flags the batch for manual review and confirmation.
* **Score $< 0.80$**: **Auto-Reject Candidate**. The runner rejects the batch, deletes the staged files, and log errors.
